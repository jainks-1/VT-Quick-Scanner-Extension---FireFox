FIREFOX SETUP GUIDE
Time: ~5 minutes

VirusTotal Setup:
1. Navigate to "https://www.virustotal.com"
2. Sign in or create an account in the top right by clicking "Sign in" or "Sign up"
3. Once logged in, click on your profile in the top right, then on "API Key"
4. Under the "API Key" box at the top, click the "Copy key to clipboard" button to the right of the blurred out text

Extension Setup:
1. Unzip the "FireFox Extension - Web Link Checker VT" folder in your computers downloads folder
2. Browse to "about:debugging" using the FireFox browser search bar
3. Click on the "This Firefox" tab on the left
4. Click the "Load Temporary Add-on..." button near the top
5. Browse to the unzipped "FireFox Extension - Web Link Checker VT" folder in your downloads folder
6. Select the "manifest.json" file, then click "open" in the bottom left
7. In your browser, click the puzzle piece icon in the top right
8. Right-click on the "Quick Link Safety Checker" and click "Pin to toolbar"
9. Click on the extension (has a magnifying glass icon) on the top right of the toolbar
10. Paste in your API key from step 4 of the VirusTotal setup
11. Click "save" then "ok"

Using The Extension
1. Right click on any link you can see on a webpage in front of you, then "Check link safety with VirusTotal"
2. Copy any link, then click the magnifying glass in the toolbar and paste it in the provided box, then click the "check link" button
3. If you feel that further investigation is required after checking a link, click the "View full report" button to be taken to the full VirusTotal report on that URL
